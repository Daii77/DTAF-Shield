About DTAF Mechanism

Adaptive Learning

DTAF continuously monitors baseline traffic to establish a dynamic threshold that adjusts in real time based on legitimate traffic patterns.

Intelligent Filtering

When traffic exceeds the adaptive threshold, the system identifies and drops malicious packets with 98% accuracy while preserving legitimate flows.

Performance Gains

DTAF reduces delay by 85% and packet loss by 95% compared to no-defense scenarios, maintaining throughput even under high-intensity DDoS attacks.

NS2 Validated

All simulation logic is based on NS2 (Network Simulator 2) methodology with mathematically accurate traffic modeling and defense algorithms.
